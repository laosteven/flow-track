## [1.0.0] - Initial Release

### Added
- Real-time stroke rate monitoring
- Stroke consistency tracking
- Advanced IMU metrics (stroke length, angles, smoothness)
- AI-powered stroke analysis
- Environmental monitoring (temperature, humidity)
- 3D trajectory visualization
- Session management and data export
- Bluetooth Low Energy connectivity
- Heat safety warnings
- Fatigue detection
- Asymmetry analysis
- Arduino Nano 33 BLE Sense Rev2 firmware
- 3D printable hardware designs

### Features
- **Real-Time Metrics**: Stroke rate, consistency, power tracking
- **Advanced IMU**: Paddle angle, smoothness, fatigue detection
- **AI Analysis**: Clean stroke detection, over-rotation warnings
- **Environmental**: Temperature/humidity monitoring, water detection
- **Visualization**: 3D motion plots, live graphs
- **Session Management**: Save, load, export training sessions
